# Data Ethics Reference - Chapter 7

## Data Sources

### World Values Survey (WVS)
- **Provider**: World Values Survey Association
- **URL**: https://www.worldvaluessurvey.org/
- **License**: Academic use permitted with citation
- **Ethics**: WVS collects data under IRB-approved protocols in participating countries

### PEW Global Attitudes Survey
- **Provider**: Pew Research Center
- **URL**: https://www.pewresearch.org/global/
- **License**: Available for research purposes with attribution
- **Ethics**: Data collected under Pew Research Center's ethical guidelines

## Usage in This Study

- Data used for aggregate analysis only
- No individual-level identifiable information processed
- Results presented at country/regional level
- Full citations provided in paper

## Original Study Ethics

The original surveys (WVS and PEW) were conducted under their respective institutional ethical approvals. This study performs secondary analysis of publicly released aggregate data.
